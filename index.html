Greninja is bipedal, frog-like Pokémon. It's movements are swift, rapid and graceful, akin to those of ninja. It evolves from Frogadier starting at level 36. It is the final form of Froakie. A Water/Dark type Pokémon. Has an alternate form: Ash-Greninja, through the process of Bond Phenomenon or Battle Bond (shown in the pokemon anime). It can compress water into various weapons.
Miraidon is a large, robotic, reptilian Pokémon with a primarily metallic, violet body. eyes resemble an LED display, with black sclerae and pupils and pixelated irises that can change pattern to simulate blinking. A dual-type Electric/Dragon Legendary Paradox Pokémon. has five different transformations, the forms are called: Low-Power Mode, Drive Mode, Aquatic Mode, Glide Mode, and Ultimate Mode. Hadron Engine (generates energy in its tail and throat, turning them into wheel-like rings for acceleration).
Suicune is a slim, quadrupedal, blue, mammalian Pokémon with white, diamond-shaped markings. it is one of the Legendary beasts resurrected by Ho-Oh after the burning of the Brass Tower. Of the three Legendary beasts, Suicune is said to represent the rains that quenched the flames of the burning Brass Tower. A Water-type Legendary Pokémon. has the power to walk across water and purify dirty water with one touch. Inner Focus (Hidden Ability)Prevents Suicune from flinching.
Zacian is a large cyan lupine Pokémon with yellow eyes. Attacks with graceful, swift, and captivating movements. Known as a legendary hero. a Fairy/steel type Legendary Pokemon Zacian has two forms: Hero of Many Battles and Crowned Sword. In Crowned Sword form, allows him to use a big golden sword (not shown in pic) in his mouth and becomes a dual type fairy/steel (In pic). In his Hero of Many Battles form, its his Original form, while using a rusted sword and is only a fairy type.
Lapras is a marine reptilian Pokémon that resembles a plesiosaur. A gentle, helpful Pokémon that enjoys ferrying people across bodies of water .A dual-type Water/Ice Pokémon Has a Form called Gigantamax (it becomes larger in size and more armored). Shell Armor (Protects Lapras from critical hits)
